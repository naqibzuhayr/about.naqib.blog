My Music World

When I was younger, I took guitar classes for a few months. It didn't last long as I dropped out of it. I liked playing the guitar but, unfortunately, it was just not for me.
Then, in my early high school year, I took drum classes. That too, did not go well and the same case happened with the guitar. Learning an instrument was just very hard for me I believe I could excel in an instrument...
but the lack of motivation, passion, and discipline dragged me out of it.

It wasn't until 2019, where I first experienced a sound that moved me in a way that my younger self would have never expected.

A sound that would shape my understanding in music, and my personality entirely.
